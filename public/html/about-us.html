About Us

Our Team BBY-32 has developed Butterfly Effect, a text-based game designed to help educate the general public on how they can contribute to climate change, with carefully researched game scenarios illustrating the impact of daily lifestyle decisions on the environment.

Our App

Vision

Our vision is to arouse awareness and educate people about this on-going serious issue, climate change, and motivate them to make small changes in their lives which can make a big difference for our better future.

Mission

Mission of Butterfly Effect is to inspire people to make choices that have less harmful effects on the environment and be part of the climate change solution.

Core Feature

Butterfly Effect offers simple text-based game with multiple choice questions related to people's daily life actions to inform them about climate change in lighthearted way.
